Wedding Vendors

Wedstimate connects the best wedding professionals with engaged couples based on the couple’s preferences.

Wedstimate is a less expensive alternative to wedding vendor aggregator sites who just show newly engaged couples every vendor in their zip code. None of these sites match vendors to couples by preference. Vendors who join Wedstimate are guaranteed at least 5 leads per month from couples that have preferences that match their services.

Vendors will receive the contact info to follow-up with the warm leads generated through the app and they can reach out to couples and book more weddings.

The fee is $50/month with discounts for purchasing a full year of service. Vendors will be guaranteed 5 warm leads every month and if their listing generates more leads they can upgrade their plan to have access to the remaining leads and to post deals that will drive more traffic to their profile.

If you are a vendor and want a less expensive way to generate more high quality leads download the Wedstimate app for free!

Wedstimate App Features

Here are a few features that make Wedstimate convenient and easy to use.

Track Your Wedding Costs

The Wedstimate app graphs your costs as you add vendors so that you can stay within your budget. It is super handy to keep costs in check and make sure that you get all the vendors that you need.

  • March 28, 2021

Vendor Percentage Match

Based on couples preferences vendors can see how close a match they are with their clients. This can be helpful for vendors evaluating what services they offer and which ones clients are seeking out.

  • February 29, 2020

Deals and Packages

Rather than paying more to be featured, vendors can add deals within Wedstimate to get more traffic to visit their profiles. This way couples who are looking for specific services can find them in more than one place!
