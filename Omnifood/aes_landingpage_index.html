Aestheticon.com — Empowering Illustrators

Aestheticon.com allows artists to post, price, and sell the usage rights of their own artwork just like a stock agency! If you would like to have more control over how much you earn for your illustrations, continue on to learn more about Aestheticon.com.

Create an account

Create an Aestheticon.com account. You will be able to set pricing, secure usage rights, post a portfolio, and track the profitability of your images.

Secure Usage Rights

Through the Aestheticon.com website you can fill out all of the appropriate paperwork and pay any related fees to securing the usage rights to your work so that your intellectual property is completely protected.

Post your portfolio

Post the images that you would like to sell as stock. Upload high-resolution images that can be directly downloaded by publishers who want to use your work.

Track your sales

Track which of your portfolio pieces are the most popular as well as what companies used them, and for what campaigns. Track what usage rights were used and how much money each publisher paid to use them.
